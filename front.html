The idea

The idea behind this project was to picture rock paper scissor in a unique way.
I imedietly thought of the idead of doing rock paper scissor in a fighting game style.

First draft

To the right you can see pictures of how the design started out. The pictures contain lo-fi prototypes of the front page and game page, in the game page you can also see my first characters. You can also see the first draft of my platform for the game.


The final design

The final design was a lot different from the original idea.
The reason for this was that I constantly was getting new ideas. Mid-project i decided to do a complete overhaul of the website using grid for the front page and flexbox for the game page. Which turned out pretty well.


How it turned out

I have to admit that I am not completely satisfied with the project. For the simple reason that I always get new ideas of improvement
